About me

Hello! My name is Violet, and I am a student at the University of Florida. I like traveling, and when I'm not studying or editing media, I like to spend time hunting for antiques.

I love creating art, in all aspects of life. Whether it's venturing into old Victorian homes to snap the perfect picture, or finding just the right edits to put in my videos, I just enjoy the satisfaction of crafting art that other people can admire and enjoy.

Above all I like to feel confident about what I'm doing, I ask questions when I need to and take pride in doing a good job.
